Welcome to my Akan Name Generator.All we need is your birthday and you will be able to see the day of the week you were born. Akan names are derived from Ghanaian culture.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a day name which corresponds to the day of the week they were born.Here are Ghanaian day names.

  • Sunday : Kwasi
  • Monday : Kwadwo
  • Tuesday :Kwabena
  • Wednesday : Kwaku
  • Thursday : Yaw
  • Friday : Kofi
  • Saturday : Kwame
  • Sunday : Akosua
  • Monday : Adwoa
  • Tuesday : Abenaa
  • Wednesday :Akua
  • Thursday : Yaa
  • Friday : Afua
  • Saturday : Ama
